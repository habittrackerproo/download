Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Habit Tracker Pro

Is Habit Tracker Pro really free?

Yes! Habit Tracker Pro is completely free forever. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium versions.

How do I save a copy to my Google Drive?

After opening the template in Google Sheets, go to File → Make a copy. This will save a copy to your Google Drive that you can edit and customize.

Can I access it from my phone?

Yes! Google Sheets works on all devices. Download the Google Sheets app, sign in with your Google account, and access your Habit Tracker from anywhere.

Can I customize the spreadsheet?

Absolutely! The spreadsheet is fully customizable. You can change colors, add or remove habits, and adjust goals to match your preferences.

Do I need a Google account?

Yes, you need a free Google account to use the Google Sheets version. If you don't have one, you can create one for free at accounts.google.com.
